Privacy Policy

Applies to PayForge, ForgeShift, ForgeTrack, WatchCue, and this website.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Scope

This policy covers PayForge, ForgeShift, ForgeTrack, the upcoming WatchCue application, and the Forge Apps support website. WatchCue is a separate product from the Forge software suite but is covered by this shared policy.

App data

Our applications are designed to minimise data collection. App data is primarily stored on your device unless a feature requires a connected service, you enable a synchronisation feature, or you explicitly choose to share information.

WatchCue

WatchCue is currently in development and is not yet publicly available. It does not currently process production app data from general users. This policy will be expanded before launch if WatchCue introduces accounts, cloud synchronisation, analytics, notifications, or any other feature that changes how personal data is handled.

Support messages

If you contact support, we receive the information you choose to include in your email, such as your name, email address, device details, screenshots, and description of the issue. We use that information only to respond to and investigate your request.

Purchases

For subscription purchases, Apple processes billing and payment data according to Apple's policies.

Policy updates

We may update this policy as applications and their features evolve. The date above indicates when the policy was most recently revised.
