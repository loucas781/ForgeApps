WatchCue

WatchCue is a simple, focused way to keep up with the shows and movies you care about. Build your personal library, track episodes as you watch them, rate favourites, and always know what is coming next.

Your watchlist, made clearer

Spend less time searching. Spend more time watching.

Keep your library, viewing progress, favourites, release calendar, and personal notes together in one focused place.

  • Track TV shows, movies, episodes, and seasons
  • Organise titles by Watching, Caught Up, On Hold, Dropped, Watchlist, and Watched
  • Browse upcoming releases in an agenda or interactive calendar
  • Back up and restore your viewing data with iCloud

WatchCue features

Everything you want to remember.

WatchCue keeps the details of your viewing life close at hand, from the next episode to the film you saved for later.

Track progress

Mark individual episodes, whole seasons, or every season as watched and see your progress at a glance.

Know what is next

Browse upcoming episodes and movie releases, then set reminders for the titles you follow.

Make it yours

Rate shows and movies, add personal notes for films, and customise layouts, checkmark colours, and detail visibility.

Your data, available

Keep saved library data available with local caching, back up and restore with iCloud, and export or import your WatchCue data.
