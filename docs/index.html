Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request help?

Email support@louisbirch.co.uk with your app name, device model, iOS version, and a short description.

How do I report a billing issue?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel via iPhone Settings > Apple Account > Subscriptions. If you still need help, contact us.

How long do support replies take?

We aim to respond within 2 business days.
